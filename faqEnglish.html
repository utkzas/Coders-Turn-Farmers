Frequently Asked Questions

What is Krish-e-हल?

Krish-e-हल is an e-commerce website that provides the farmers an aggregated platform to rent-out or rent-in tools and facilitates the transaction between fellow users.

Am I eligible to be a user of Krish-e?

Any Indian farmer with authentic Aadhar Card is eligible to be a part of Krish-e family.

How do I become a member of Krish-e?

Go to "link", and enter your Name, Aadhar number and Phone number. You will receive an OTP once verification is done. Enter the OTP and click on Proceed. Your account will be created and you will be redirected to the dashboard.

How to rent tools?

Once on the dashboard, you will find 4 options there:

  • Profile - You will find your personal details, and your rent history in here.
  • Rent-in - Here, you can find the tools you need for farming with tools details, price and location.
  • Rent-out - Here, you can rent-out the tools that you don't need at the moment. All you have to do is click a picture of your tool, and fill in the necessary details.
  • Advisory - Farming is one of the toughest and most important occupation, and hence, Krish-e us here to make it a little easy for you. All you have to do is put in your question related to farming or the website. Our experts will soon get in touch with you and help you with the solution.
